## Usage Instructions

1. **Choose your character concept** first (warrior, mystic, royal, etc.)
2. **Select variables** from each category that match your vision
3. **Fill in the template** with your chosen options
4. **Adjust and refine** based on first results
5. **Add negative prompts** if needed to avoid unwanted elements

## Pro Tips

- **For flowing fabric**: Emphasize movement keywords in [Fabric Movement]
- **For static poses**: Use "static drape" and "controlled lines"
- **For period accuracy**: Be specific in [Style] and [Cultural Fusion]
- **Color harmony**: Choose palette type in addition to specific colors
- Do NOT change the `Pose` and `Photography` section until user asks

## Notes
- Keep `Art Style` as `photorealistic` unless asked.
- Directly output the result without any quote like "```".
